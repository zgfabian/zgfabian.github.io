_Apano Stin Triandafillia_ (Απάνω Στην Τριανταφυλλιά - often referred shortly _Apano Stin_) means is "Upon the rose bush". As many of Greek and other folksongs (Dimotika - Δημοτικά) is about feeling of love symbolized by the nature.
